CLAUSE 5 – CONDITION AND CARE OF PROPERTY

5.1 The Tenant must keep the Property in a reasonably clean and tidy condition throughout the tenancy.

5.2 The Tenant must return the Property to the Landlord at the end of the tenancy in a similar condition to that in which it was provided at the commencement of the tenancy, fair wear and tear excepted.

5.3 The Tenant is responsible for any damage beyond fair wear and tear caused by the Tenant, members of the Tenant's household, or visitors.

---

CLAUSE 6 – CLEANING

6.1 The Tenant must leave the Property in a clean condition at the end of the tenancy.

6.2 If the Property requires additional cleaning beyond normal standards at the end of the tenancy, the reasonable cost of such cleaning may be deducted from the deposit.

Note: Any clause requiring the Tenant to pay for professional cleaning regardless of the condition of the Property at check-out shall be unenforceable under the Tenant Fees Act 2019.

---

CLAUSE 7 – DECORATION AND DAMAGE

7.1 The Tenant must not carry out any redecoration without the prior written consent of the Landlord.

7.2 Minor marks and scuffs consistent with normal habitation and fair wear and tear are not considered damage.

7.3 The Tenant is responsible for damage beyond fair wear and tear, including burns, stains, large holes in walls, or significant damage to fixtures and fittings.

---

CLAUSE 8 – DEPOSIT

8.1 The Landlord holds a deposit of £1,200 as security against unpaid rent, damage beyond fair wear and tear, cleaning required beyond normal standards, and missing items.

8.2 The deposit will be protected in accordance with the Housing Act 2004.

8.3 At the end of the tenancy, the Landlord will return the deposit less any agreed deductions within 10 days of both parties agreeing the amount.

8.4 If the parties cannot agree, either party may refer the dispute to the relevant deposit protection scheme's Alternative Dispute Resolution service.

---

CLAUSE 9 – REPAIRS

9.1 The Landlord is responsible for structural repairs, heating, hot water, and gas and electrical installations.

9.2 The Tenant must report any damage or disrepair to the Landlord promptly.

9.3 The Tenant must not carry out repairs without prior written consent of the Landlord.

---

CLAUSE 12 – GARDENS AND OUTSIDE SPACES

12.1 The Tenant must keep any garden, yard, or outside space in a reasonably tidy condition.
